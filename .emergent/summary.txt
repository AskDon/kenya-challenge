**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build The Kenya Challenge, a web and mobile-friendly application for the charity KEF (Kenya Education Fund). The application's goal is to facilitate virtual walking challenges along Kenyan routes combined with peer-to-peer fundraising.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **User Roles:** Walker, Teammate, Supporter (formerly Sponsor), Corporate Sponsor, and Admin.
- **Authentication:** Email/password-based authentication.
- **Challenges:** Admins can define challenges with names, descriptions, distances, and milestones.
- **Walker Types:** Admins can define different tiers for walkers to join, each with a specific cost (e.g., Basic 5, Builder 7).
- **Achievement Levels:** Admins can define fundraising milestones based on the total amount raised by a walker and their supporters, with corresponding swag.
- **Teams:** Walkers can create teams and invite teammates via a unique link.
- **Activity Logging:** Walkers can manually log their daily activity in steps or kilometers.
- **Fundraising:** Each walker gets a unique public fundraising page to collect pledges from supporters. Supporters can pledge a flat amount or per kilometer.
- **Leaderboards:** Public pages showing top individuals and teams by distance and funds raised.
- **Admin Console:** A simple interface for admins to perform CRUD operations on challenges, walker types, achievement levels, and view summary statistics.
- **Design:** A clean, mobile-first design inspired by the KEF website, using the The Kenya Challenge brand placeholders and KEF's color accents.

**User's preferred language**: English

A multi-step onboarding wizard guides new users through selecting a challenge, walker type, and team. The UI has been customized with the KEF logo and brand colors.
